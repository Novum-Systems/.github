# Code of Conduct

## 1. Purpose

This Code of Conduct defines the expected standards of behavior for all participants contributing to or interacting with projects within the Novum Systems organization.

Our goal is to maintain a professional, respectful, and productive environment for all contributors.

---

## 2. Expected Behavior

All participants are expected to:

- Communicate respectfully and constructively
- Provide clear, relevant, and thoughtful feedback
- Focus on improving the quality of the project
- Be open to discussion, review, and iteration
- Act in a professional and collaborative manner

---

## 3. Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not permitted:

- Harassment, discrimination, or offensive language
- Personal attacks or hostile interactions
- Disruptive or unproductive behavior in discussions
- Misrepresentation of work or intent
- Any conduct that negatively impacts collaboration

---

## 4. Contribution Standards

Contributors are expected to:

- Follow established workflows and guidelines
- Respect project structure and decisions
- Ensure contributions are relevant and constructive
- Avoid unnecessary or low-quality submissions

---

## 5. Enforcement

Maintainers of the repository reserve the right to:

- Reject or request changes to contributions
- Limit or remove access to collaboration
- Take appropriate action in response to violations

---

## 6. Reporting Issues

If you experience or observe behavior that violates this Code of Conduct:

- Report it through appropriate channels (e.g., repository owner)
- Provide clear and relevant context

All reports will be handled with discretion.

---

## 7. Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All repositories within the Novum Systems organization
- All forms of interaction, including issues, pull requests, and discussions

---

## 8. Philosophy

Novum Systems prioritizes:

- Clarity over complexity  
- Structure over chaos  
- Constructive collaboration over conflict  

All interactions should reflect these principles.
